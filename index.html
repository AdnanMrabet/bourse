How pricing works

Bourse prices ideas on the strength of the argument, never on the size of a wallet.

  1. List. Any idea opens as a tradable asset at a flat one hundred. No deposit, no custody.

  2. Pitch. You argue instead of buy. A bull or bear thesis is the order; the reasoning is the size.

  3. Rule. An on-chain AI Analyst weighs the case and validators must agree before the price re-prices.
