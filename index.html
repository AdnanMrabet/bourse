The mechanism

How the market re-prices on argument

  1. 01

    List an idea

    Anyone can open a market on an idea. It lists as a tradable asset at a flat starting price of one hundred. No deposit, no custody, just a name and a conviction that it matters.

  2. 02

    Pitch a thesis

    Rather than buying, you make a case. Write a bull or bear thesis on the idea. The argument is the order, and the strength of the reasoning is the size of the position.

  3. 03

    The Analyst rules

    An on-chain AI Analyst weighs the thesis on its merits and rules bullish, bearish, or neutral with a magnitude. Validators independently re-run the call and must agree before the price re-prices on chain.
